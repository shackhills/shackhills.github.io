K and S Mints Ltd.
Bringing quality products at affordable prices
Focuses on health by supplying fresh, natural, non-toxic peppermints at an affordable cost
Quality customer service as they can contact us at any time.
Aims to establish a sustainable business in growing peppermints and manufacturing peppermint products such as peppermint oils, which is a highly imported product.
One of the major initiatives of K & S Mints is a consumer health initiative, individuals have a desire to get stronger and healthier.
K & S Mints initiative focuses on health by supplying natural and non-toxic peppermints, at an affordable price, to help maintain one's health.
K&S Mints produces peppermints through the use of a hydroponic system.
The company stays abreast with any new developments in the agricultural sector on a regional and global scale.
We currently retail to caterers, tea shops, hair care specialists, retail buyers who require peppermints for tea/seasoning and a few small massage businesses who request leaves to make peppermint oil.
K&S mints offer deals upon purchases over 1 pound as compared to our competitors who offer one fixed price.
One of KandS Mints goal is to build more hydroponic systems to sustainably meet the needs of our current clientele and further expand the reaches of our business.
We have locations in Moruga and Cocoyea
