K and S Mints Ltd.

Hi, thanks for visiting the site!

This business aims to provide high quality peppermints at affordable prices to each and every entity in Trinidad and Tobago that uses them for their services. Eventually once Trinidad and Tobago needs are met the business has plans to export products to the regional and eventually global market.

Kendall Bryon is highly experienced in building hydroponic systems, planting different types of crops and he possess the fundamental knowledge that enables the planting and growing of the mint plants at its best quality. He also successfully markets the products which encourages a continuous flow of income.

Shaquille Hills manages the administrative work of the business such as financial statements, invoices, expenditure. He also has experience in using lean management techniques to perform the duties effectively and efficiently.
